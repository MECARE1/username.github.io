Western Line

The Western Line connects Churchgate to Dahanu Road, serving the southern part of the city to the far northern suburbs.

Central Line

The Central Line links South Mumbai to the eastern suburbs, connecting CST to Thane.

Harbour Line

The Harbour Line connects CST to Navi Mumbai, providing seamless connectivity to areas such as Vashi and Panvel.
